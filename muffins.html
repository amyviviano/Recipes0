Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1.5 cup
Granulated Sugar 0.5 cup
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Salt 0.5 tsp
Egg 1
Margarine 0.5 cup
Mashed Banana 1.5 cup
Chocolate Chips 300 grams

Instructions

  1. Whisk 5 dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Beat egg, melted margarine, and mashed bananas in medium bowl.
  3. Add banana mixture to dry ingredients, stirring until moistened.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Fill muffin cups or sprayed muffin pan
  6. Bake @ 350F for 15 - 20 minutes until tops are firm to touch.
